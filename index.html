What is Para Legal?

A **paralegal** is a **motivated legal professional** who provides essential support to a licensed advocate, performing substantial legal work like research, document drafting, and case management.

Serving as the **key link for daily interactions**, the paralegal ensures seamless communication and information flow between the senior advocate and the client regarding case status, documentation, and deadlines.

Ultimately, this specialized support allows the advocate to concentrate solely on strategy and courtroom representation while guaranteeing the client receives consistent, organized updates and reliable administrative service.
